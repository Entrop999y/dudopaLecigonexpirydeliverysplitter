Duodopa / Lecigon delivery split calculator

Checks whether picked cassette stock will still be in date by the time the patient reaches it, based on delivery date and daily dose. If not, tells you exactly how many packs to swap for later-dated stock.

1Delivery & dose

Total packs required is calculated from cover days × dose ÷ cassettes per pack (rounded up). Override it manually if the order quantity differs.

2Picked stock, earliest expiry first

Add a row for each expiry date in the picked stock (e.g. a short-dated batch and a longer-dated batch). Any shortfall against the total required is assumed fillable from ample-dated shelf stock.
